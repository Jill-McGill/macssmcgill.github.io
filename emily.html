U1 Representative Emily Clark

Hi! My name is Emily and I am one of three U1 reps for the 2016-2017 year. I’m from the GIANT town of Dorchester, Ontario, home to... honestly, just a lot of cows. This is my second year at McGill and I'm loving this city more and more everyday.

You'll most likely find me making a fool of myself in front of ANAT 261/262, pulling my hair out in McLennan or just shovelling Lola Rosa into my mouth. No matter what I’m doing/eating, come chat me up and we can talk some serious anatomy and cell biology. You can also find me at the MACSS office for office hours or shoot me an email at u1.macss@gmail.com.

-Emily
